You Asked

Common Questions

What should I expect during my first visit? +

Your first visit begins with a private 15-minute skin consultation. We assess your skin type, concerns, and goals before recommending and beginning your bespoke treatment. Everything is tailored — nothing is generic.

Are your products safe for sensitive skin? +

Yes. All LUMÉ formulations are dermatologist-tested, free from parabens, sulphates, and synthetic fragrances. We carry a dedicated sensitive-skin range and our therapists are trained to adapt every treatment accordingly.

How long do treatment results last? +

Results vary by treatment. Most facials deliver visible radiance for 2–4 weeks. For lasting transformation, we recommend a monthly ritual programme. Your therapist will advise the ideal frequency for your skin during consultation.

Can I purchase LUMÉ products without a treatment? +

Absolutely. Our full product range is available for purchase directly. We recommend a brief consultation to ensure you receive the right formulations for your skin — but it is entirely optional.

Where are you located in Abuja? +

Our studio is located in Abuja, Nigeria. Exact address is shared upon booking confirmation via WhatsApp. We are conveniently situated in a serene, private setting designed for your comfort and calm.
